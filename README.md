# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview
An end-to-end data analytics project on Zomato's restaurant dataset to uncover insights on pricing, ratings, cuisine trends, service availability, and city-wise restaurant distribution. The goal was to help business teams identify what's working, where opportunities exist, and how the market is evolving.

---

|---|---|

| Python (Pandas) | Data cleaning and transformation |
| Excel | Data standardization, validation, Excel Dashboard |
| Power BI | Interactive KPI dashboard and visual analytics |

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Analyze distribution of restaurants by price, rating, city, and country
- Study availability of services like online delivery and table booking
- Identify most popular cuisines and cities
- Track restaurant growth trends over time
- Discover top-performing cuisines and high-potential markets

---

## 📊 KPIs Tracked
- Total Restaurants, Cuisines & Countries covered
- % of restaurants offering Table Booking
- % of restaurants offering Online Delivery
- Average Customer Ratings by city and country
- Restaurant opening trends over time
- Price range distribution across segments

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- 🏷️ **54% of restaurants** fall in the budget category (under ₹500) — mass market dominates
- 🚗 Only **~25% of restaurants** offer online delivery — significant growth opportunity
- 🍛 **North Indian and Chinese** cuisines are the most popular in major cities
- ⭐ Majority of restaurants rated **3.5 and above**, indicating good overall quality
- 📍 Major cities lead in restaurant count, but **smaller cities are growing fast**
- 📅 Restaurant launches spike during **busy seasons**, indicating market timing opportunities

---

## 📋 Recommendations
1. **Targeted Cuisine Expansion** — Onboard more restaurants in high-demand cuisines (North Indian, Chinese)
2. **Boost Online Delivery Adoption** — Only 25% offer delivery; huge untapped market
3. **Service Improvement** — Focus on lower-rated cities to improve delivery efficiency and quality
4. **Geographic Expansion** — Target cities/countries with fewer restaurants but growing demand
5. **Promotional Campaigns** — Run localized promotions based on city-specific dining habits
